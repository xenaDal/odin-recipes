Okra in the oven (Mπάμιες στο φούρνο)

The recipe below is not my own. Click the link to view it on the original page.

Description

Okra is a versatile vegetable that can be eaten in many ways, filled with lots of seeds and a quite hardy outside. They are very easy to cook with and can be included in many dishes to add nutrition and flavour.

This dish is very easy to put together, with everything going in one tray in the oven. You won't regret it!

Ingredients:

  • 1kg okra
  • 2 medium potatoes, cut in 8
  • 2 medium carrots, cut in 6
  • 2 tbs tomato paste
  • 3 large ripe tomatoes, chopped or replace with 1 1/2 cups pasatta
  • 1 medium brown onion, thinly sliced
  • 10 sprigs of parsley or coriander, dependent on preference
  • 3/4 cup of olive oil
  • 3/4 cup white wine vinegar, red wine vinegar may be used also
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt or as per desired amount
  • 1/4 tsp pepper

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven at 180°C. Wash the okra and pat dry.
  2. Mix all the ingredients together in a baking tray. Cover with with foil or lid and place it for 1 hour and 30min in the oven. The last 15min remove the lid to allow the food to get a bit of colour.
  3. Enjoy warm with a fresh salad, yoghurt and crusty bread to dip into the tomato sauce!
